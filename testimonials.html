üå¨ CPAP - Continuous Positive Airway Pressure

CPAP is a medical therapy that uses a machine to deliver pressurized air through a mask, ensuring the airway remains open and preventing breathing interruptions during sleep. It is primarily employed to treat Hypopnea, Apnea, and other respiratory disorders.

Benefits of CPAP

Improves overall quality of life  :   Helps individuals with sleep disorders feel more refreshed and energetic.

Lowers health risks  :   Decreases the likelihood of heart disease, high blood pressure, and stroke.

Improves sleep quality :   Ensures uninterrupted breathing, leading to better rest.

Reduces snoring :   Keeps the airway open, minimizing snoring.
